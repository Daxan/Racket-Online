Welcome to Try Racket - a quick tour of Racket for absolute beginners.

Here is our only disclaimer: this site is an introduction to Racket, not a Racket REPL. You won't be able to do everything in it that you could do in your local interpreter.

"Try Racket" is written in Racket and JavaScript with Chris Done's jquery-console. It borrows a lot of code and content from Try Clojure and paste.rkt. Hosting is provided by John Berry, who is also the current maintainer of the project.
